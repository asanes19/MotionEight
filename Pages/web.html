Our Projects

Bait Albahar - Landing Page

On the homepage of this website dedicated to luxurious furnished properties in the Al Rashidiya area of Ajman, the brand logo is prominently displayed along with a concise description that reflects the excellence and luxury of the offered apartments. The page also features links to social media platforms such as Snapchat, Instagram, and TikTok, along with a contact phone number for visitor communication. Additionally, there's a button provided for switching between the English and Arabic languages to cater to different user preferences. The page aims to capture attention and guide visitors to other sections of the website for more information and exploration.

Asanes Used Cars Showroom

The project consists of a web application with two parts: a user-friendly website and a powerful management system, both designed for a used car showroom. On the website, customers can easily see the cars available for sale, learn about the showroom, sell their cars, request specific models, and join car auctions. The website also offers cool features like a Virtual Test Drive and a 360 Interior Tour, making the browsing experience exciting.

Flcon Island - Landing Page

The Falcon Island Website is where you can learn all about our special project. It's like a digital home for information and pictures. You can explore different parts of the project, like the fancy townhouses, beautiful views, and how everything is planned. There's even a video tour and 3D plans to help you imagine being there. And if you want to know more or have questions, you can easily get in touch with us. This website shows how much we care about making the project exciting and easy to understand for everyone interested.

Khodari Used Cars Showroom

The "Khodari Used Cars Showroom Web App" provides easy access to car information across three branches: Mohamed, Ahmed, and Haeean. Users can explore inventory, view car details, and connect with showrooms and social media. An admin panel streamlines inventory and user management. The app enhances car browsing for potential buyers, effectively bridging the gap with Khodari showroom.
